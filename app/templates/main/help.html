Getting Started

Creating an Account
  1. Click on "Sign Up" in the navigation menu
  2. Enter your IITK email address, name, and other required information
  3. Create a secure password
  4. Verify your email with the OTP sent to your IITK email
  5. Complete your profile
Navigating the Platform
  • Home: Overview of the platform
  • Buy/Get: Browse available items
  • Sell/Donate: List your items
  • My Items: Manage your listed items
  • Wishlist: View saved items
  • Buying Requests: Manage your buying and selling requests

Buying Guide

Finding Items

Use the search and filter options on the Buy/Get page to find specific items:

  • Search by keyword
  • Filter by category
  • Set price range
  • Filter by condition
  • Show only free items or bargainable items
  • Sort by newest, oldest, or price
Contacting Givers
  1. Express interest in an item
  2. Wait for the giver to accept your request
  3. Once accepted, you'll see the giver's contact information
  4. Contact the giver via WhatsApp, call, or email to arrange a meeting
Meeting and Transaction Tips
  • Meet in public places on campus (e.g., hostel common areas, canteens)
  • Inspect the item thoroughly before paying
  • Use secure payment methods (UPI, cash)
  • Be punctual for meetings
  • Communicate clearly about any issues or concerns

Selling Guide

Creating Effective Listings
  • Use clear, descriptive titles
  • Select the appropriate category
  • Be honest about the condition
  • Provide detailed specifications
  • Set a fair price (or mark as donation)
  • Take clear, well-lit photos from multiple angles
Managing Taker Requests
  1. Check your notifications
  2. Review requests
  3. Accept or decline requests
  4. Communicate promptly with takers

Account Management

Resetting Your Password
  1. Click "Login" in the navigation menu
  2. Click "Forgot Password?" link
  3. Enter your IITK email
  4. Check your email for the OTP
  5. Enter the OTP and your new password
Managing Your Listings
  • View all your listings on the "My Items" page
  • Mark items as unavailable once sold
  • Remove listings if needed

Safety Tips

Personal Safety
  • Always meet in public places on campus
  • Bring a friend for high-value transactions
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, walk away
  • Don't share unnecessary personal information
Transaction Safety
  • Inspect items thoroughly before paying
  • Use secure payment methods (UPI, cash)
  • Don't make advance payments without seeing the item
  • Keep records of your transactions

Campus Etiquette

Respectful Communication
  • Be polite and respectful in all communications
  • Respond to messages promptly
  • Be clear about your intentions
  • Don't spam givers with lowball offers
Meeting Etiquette
  • Be punctual for meetings
  • Inform the other party if you're running late
  • If you need to cancel, give as much notice as possible
  • Be prepared with the exact amount for payment
